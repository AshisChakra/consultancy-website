My Story

My journey into personal finance and self-help consulting began with my own struggles. After experiencing financial difficulties while battling depression, I realized the profound connection between financial stability and mental health.

"Financial stress and mental health challenges often feed into each other, creating a cycle that can feel impossible to break. My mission is to help people address both sides of this equation."

I pursued education in finance and psychology, developing a unique approach that addresses both the practical aspects of financial management and the emotional barriers that often prevent progress.

Having successfully helped numerous clients in West Bengal navigate their financial challenges while improving their mental well-being, I established my consulting practice to reach more individuals in need of comprehensive support.

Today, I work with clients from various backgrounds, providing personalized strategies that respect their unique circumstances and goals. I'm particularly passionate about making financial guidance accessible and creating stigma-free spaces to discuss mental health challenges.
