How It Works

My consulting process is designed to be straightforward and focused on your needs. Here's what you can expect when working with me:

1

Initial Consultation

We'll start with a complimentary 30-minute consultation to discuss your needs and determine if we're a good fit for working together. This can be done in person (in West Bengal) or via video call.

2

Assessment & Planning

If we decide to move forward, I'll conduct a comprehensive assessment of your current situation, whether financial, emotional, or both. Based on this, we'll develop a personalized action plan.

3

Implementation Support

I'll provide guidance and support as you implement the strategies we've developed. This includes practical tools, resources, and regular check-ins to help you stay on track.

4

Review & Adjustment

We'll regularly review your progress and make adjustments to the plan as needed. I believe in flexibility and adapting strategies as your circumstances change.
